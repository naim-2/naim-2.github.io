My Story

Building things that matter

Hi, I'm Naim Salim — a Full Stack & Mobile Software Developer and a 2024 Magna Cum Laude graduate from United States International University – Africa, where I earned my BSc in Applied Computer Technology. I specialize in Flutter, Firebase, Cloudflare Workers, serverless backend development, M-Pesa integration, and modern cloud technologies.

I enjoy building complete production systems — from concept to deployment — and transforming ideas into reliable, user-centered applications. My work spans React, Vue, Python, machine learning, Azure, and Oracle Cloud. I'm passionate about creating intelligent, scalable applications that merge AI, mobile engineering, and cloud-based automation.

Some of my most impactful work includes NAVI, a mobile AI assistant for the visually impaired that passed Stage 1 of the Microsoft Imagine Cup 2024, and LUMEA, a booking and M-Pesa payment system built using Flutter and Cloudflare Workers — two projects that reflect both technical depth and genuine social purpose.

Outside of software, I'm a dedicated competitive chess player. I've competed in the Kenya Premier Chess League for three seasons — representing Mombasa Chess Academy (2018–2019) and USIU-Africa (2023–2024). In 2019, I represented Kenya at the Africa Youth Chess Championship in Windhoek, Namibia, finishing 14th out of 22 in the U18 category. Chess has sharpened my analytical thinking, pattern recognition, and strategic focus — all qualities that directly enhance my engineering work.

I also practice origami, a hobby that complements my attention to structural detail. As I continue growing in my career, I'm excited to collaborate with teams passionate about software engineering, mobile development, AI, and cloud innovation.
