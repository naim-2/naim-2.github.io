About Me

Hi, I'm Naim Salim, a Full Stack & Mobile Software Developer and a 2024 graduate from United States International University – Africa, where I earned my BSc in Applied Computer Technology, graduating magna cum laude. I specialize in Flutter, Firebase, Cloudflare Workers, serverless backend development, M-Pesa integration, and modern cloud technologies. I enjoy building complete production systems—from concept to deployment—and transforming ideas into reliable, user-centered applications.

I have experience working with a wide range of technologies, including React, Vue, Python, machine learning, Azure, and Oracle Cloud. I’m passionate about creating intelligent, scalable, and modern applications that merge AI, mobile engineering, and cloud-based automation. Some of my most impactful work includes NAVI, a mobile assistant for the visually impaired, and LUMEA, a booking and M-Pesa payment system built using Flutter and Cloudflare Workers.

Outside of software development, I am a dedicated competitive chess player. I have competed in the Kenya Premier Chess League for three seasons—representing Mombasa Chess Academy (2018–2019) and USIU-Africa (2023–2024). In 2019, I represented Kenya at the Africa Youth Chess Championship (AYCC) in Windhoek, Namibia, where I scored 4 wins, 1 draw, and 4 losses, finishing 14th out of 22 in the U18 category. Chess has strengthened my analytical thinking, discipline, and strategic focus—qualities that directly enhance my work as a developer.

I also enjoy origami, a creativity-driven hobby that complements my attention to detail. As I continue growing in my career, I’m excited to connect with teams and individuals passionate about software development, mobile engineering, AI, and modern cloud technologies—while contributing to innovative projects and continuously improving my skills.
